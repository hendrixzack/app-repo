    Repository: https://github.com/DJ322

  • Project Outline

    Description: Everett Enterprises is a marketing agency for major social media companies. They currently host their business in a on-premises data center, but have decided to move to the cloud due to the financial and performance improvements. They have a website that is utilized by their clients, and it is urgent that they have a working environment for their business requirements.

    We were hire to assist their company with the transition. We designed a fast, scalable, reliable, and cost-effective solution to replace their on-premises infrastructure.

    For this project, we build a multi-tier architecture in AWS utilizing DevOps tools such as Terraform and deploy the application and infrastructure using Jenkins.
